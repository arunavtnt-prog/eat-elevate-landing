Frequently asked questions

How does the coaching work?

After onboarding, we match you with a coach based on your goals and training style. They build your program, review every session you log, watch your form videos, and adjust your plan weekly. Think of it as having a trainer in your pocket — minus the $150/hour rate.

Do i need a gym?

No, your coach designs your plan based on the equipment you have available. Whether you train at a full gym or home, your movement blueprints will adapt perfectly.

Is there form feedback?

Yes, you can upload videos of your exercises directly through the app. Your coach will analyze your technique and send back specific cues to help you improve your form.

Can i change my plan?

Life happens, so your coach can adjust your weekly schedule at any time. Just send an SMS to update your workouts for travel, busy days, or low energy levels.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 7-day free trial so you can experience the coaching first. You can explore the features and chat with your trainer before committing to a full plan.

When will i see results?

Consistency is the key to seeing progress with your 360° fitness roadmap. Most members notice improved energy and strength gains within the first four weeks of dedicated training.
